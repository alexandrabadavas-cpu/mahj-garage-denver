Table Info

Not sure which session is right for you? Here's the breakdown.

Beginner Lessons

Hands-on, small group lessons where you'll learn the rules of the game through a mix of instruction and guided play.

Guided Play

For those who know the basics but still want an instructor by their side to help with rules, answer questions, and talk strategy.

Open Play

You know the game, you know the rules, and you just want to get your mahj on and play some games!
